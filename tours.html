Routes;

Routh One. New York
18th-23rd. June 2018-6 Days 5 Night
New York (18th June) – Arrival at Kota Kinabalu followed by dinner to meet fellow riders , followed by a briefing of the course, safety and the ride.

Routh Two. Chicago
18th-23rd. June 2018-6 Days 5 Night
Chicago (19th June) – Gears in the support vehicles . Ride from Kota Kinabalu to Tanjung Aru Station (not far from Tanjung Aru Beach) . Start of ride to Papar then to Sipitang. Approximate distance 170km. The ride is relatively flat with slight undulation. Riders who are unable to complete the ride can hop onto one of the support vehicles.

Routh Three. Mexico
18th-23rd. June 2018-6 Days 5 Night
Mexico – (20th June) Sipitang – Tenom Depart by 7.30am to Tenom, Padas Farm Stay. 68km Undulating hills. Check into Padas Farm stay. Lunch and Dinner Provided.

Routh Four. Wales
18th-23rd. June 2018-6 Days 5 Night
Wales – (21st June) Tenom – Tambunan The hills comes ALIVE! Crocker Hills @Banjaran Crocker if you still remember your geography during school days, undulating for sure! 110km only to Tambunan Village Resort.

Routh Five. London
18th-23rd. June 2018-6 Days 5 Night
London – (22nd June) Tambunan –Kota Kinabalu 78km to the City.
